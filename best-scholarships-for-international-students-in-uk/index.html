Established in 1903, the prestigious Rhodes Scholarship is one of the oldest and most prestigious international scholarships awarded to students from selected countries worldwide, including the UK. This fully funded scholarship allows exceptional postgraduate students to study at the University of Oxford.

  • Eligibility: Open to students from select countries, must be between the ages of 19-25, and must have completed their undergraduate degree with a minimum GPA of 3.7.
