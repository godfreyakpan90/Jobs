Sales Analyst

We are looking for a talented SALES ANALYST to join our team specializing in SALES for our POWER SYSTEMS BUSINESS SEGMENT in ACCRA, GHANA. In this role, the candidate will make an impact in the following ways:

  • Gathers & analyzes market demand from distributors and provides insights for leaders to finalize the region forecast
  • Uses tools & reporting databases to draw conclusions from the data for decision-making purposes.
  • Consolidates and manages sales projections for volumes and financial implications in both the monthly demand planning process and delivers to the Strategic process while working cross-functionally to support the annual operating planning process.
  • Fosters/aids/execute the development of new methods, processes, and procedures
  • Supports profitable growth or new business opportunities jointly with the key accounts and supports cost initiatives
  • Maintains accurate reporting, forecasting, and records through the utilization of Cummins tools and processes

Qualifications to be successful in this role, the candidate will need the following:

  • Advanced Excel Skills and Power BI experience are needed to deal with large sets of data
  • 3 to 5 years of Experience in Demand Planning and financial skills
  • The ability to communicate or interact well with other people, listening skills, and presentation skills
  • Project management skills to manage assignments while collaborating with stakeholders in different time zones

Primary Location Ghana-Greater Accra-Accra-Ghana, Accra, Cummins Distributor
