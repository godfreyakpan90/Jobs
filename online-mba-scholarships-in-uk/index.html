The Master of Business Administration (MBA) is a highly sought-after academic program with worldwide recognition. In many countries, like the United Kingdom, MBA courses are available through both business schools and universities. While several universities globally offer MBA programs, not all of them currently provide online MBA options. Explore the benefits of online MBA programs and broaden your educational horizons today!

#1. Online MBA Scholarship at Purdue University

The Krannert Executive MBA programs offer excellent returns on investment compared to other top-ranked EMBA programs. With competitive pricing, ample scholarship options, and diverse financial aid choices, these programs make it accessible for anyone to pursue a Krannert Executive MBA. Students can fund their education through a variety of sources, including program and external scholarships, federal and private student loans, personal income or savings, and corporate sponsorship. While not all students are eligible for employer financial support, corporate sponsorship is not mandatory for program admission. Start your journey towards a Krannert Executive MBA today!

#2. Online MBA Scholarships at Wake Forest University

Looking to advance your career? Wake Forest University offers a top-notch 20-month MBA program designed for working professionals in North Carolina. With campuses in Uptown Charlotte and Winston-Salem, our flexible program offers evening and weekend classes, as well as the convenient Online MBA option. Enhance your business acumen with our well-regarded program, which provides a strategic, worldwide outlook and equips you to tackle challenges from diverse angles.

Our Evening MBA program also offers diverse scholarship options, including endowed awards and competitive scholarships. These scholarships are determined based on the information gathered from your admissions application, eliminating the need for a separate application. Check out the external scholarship opportunities below for more details.

Join us at Wake Forest University and take your career to new heights with our comprehensive MBA program. Learn more today!

There are various online MBA options at Wake Forest University which includes;

Yellow Ribbon Program

  • Wake Forest University School of Business collaborates with the Yellow Ribbon GI Education Enhancement campaign, aiding veterans and eligible dependents in covering tuition expenses.
  • The School of Business Enrollment Management Center oversees the program, which extends to various Wake Forest graduate business programs, including MBA, MSA, and MSM degrees.
  • The Yellow Ribbon campaign, supported by the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs, offers matching scholarship funds as recognition of military service for post-9/11 veterans.
  • Veterans meeting the 100% eligibility criteria for the Yellow Ribbon program can receive up to $15,500 in matching funds per academic year from the University.

Military MBA Partnership

  • Wake Forest is a Military MBA partner school, offering support to military officers seeking an MBA for career progression.
  • Recognized as military-friendly, Wake Forest University values the achievements of military students returning to pursue graduate business degrees at the School of Business.
  • MBA graduates with previous military service are in high demand, often receiving multiple job offers with competitive compensation packages.
  • The School of Business boasts military veterans in leadership positions, including the Dean of Business, Executive Director of the Evening MBA program, and numerous faculty members, who appreciate the unique advantages brought by students with military experience.
  • The Military Student Association also plays a vital role in aiding MBA students and their spouses as they transition to Wake Forest.

#3. Online MBA Scholarships at Villanova University

Villanova School of Business is excited to extend merit scholarships to high-achieving students. Our comprehensive evaluation takes into account various factors such as academic performance, professional accomplishments, coursework rigor, GPA, and test scores. Scholarship decisions are made as part of the admissions process. In the event that you are not granted a scholarship, you have the option to complete a Scholarship Request Form on the Application Status Page. The review process involves assessing eligibility and entails an additional essay. Typically, scholarship decisions are reached within one week of form submission. If you have any inquiries regarding the scholarship application process, please don’t hesitate to reach out to our admissions representatives.

Villanova University offers Online MBA Scholarships, enabling professionals to earn their degree in just two years. Our flexible and personalized online format provides the prestigious Villanova MBA experience. Choose from a range of specializations including Analytics, Finance, International Business, Strategic Management, and Marketing.

#4. Online MBA Scholarships at the University of California

The Online MBA program at the University of California offers a groundbreaking opportunity to access a top-ranked management school within the UC system. With MBA@UCDavis, you can earn a high-quality graduate business degree from UC Davis, regardless of your location.

Our program emphasizes the innovative use of data in making business decisions, a highly sought-after skill in the Northern California and Silicon Valley regions. Experience live virtual classes, engaging in lively discussions with peers and world-class faculty through our cutting-edge technology platform.

Explore various financing options for your MBA program, including student loans, scholarships, corporate sponsorships, and military education benefits, offered by the University of California.

Admitted students in the January 2022 and April 2022 cohorts can request a deferral for the latter half of their program tuition, up to $52,740, interest-free. Don’t miss this unique opportunity!

Elevate your career with the transformative MBA@UCDavis program. Join us today!

Benefits of Online MBA Scholarships at University of California

  • Corporate sponsorship

Certain companies offer full or partial financial assistance for their employees’ tuition. To explore this option, it is advisable to research your company’s specific policies or get in touch with your company’s human resources representative to inquire about available opportunities and the corresponding application procedures.

  • Federal student loans

U.S. citizens and permanent residents have the eligibility to seek the Direct Unsubsidized Loan and the credit-based Direct Graduate PLUS Loan.

  • Private loans and scholarships

Numerous private organizations provide scholarship and loan options, which might necessitate individual applications.

  • Military education benefits

Eligible students have the option to investigate the use of education benefits available through the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs. Veterans may also access benefits provided by the UC Davis Veterans Success Center. It’s important to note that students in the online MBA program are not eligible for CalVet benefits.

  • Deferred Tuition Plan

An innovative payment option introduced for admitted students in the January 2022 and April 2022 cohorts. This plan enables students to postpone tuition payments for the latter part of the program, with the potential to defer up to $52,740 at a 0% interest rate. For additional information, please visit our Deferred Tuition Plan page.

  • UC Davis Scholarships

Every applicant is automatically evaluated for merit-based scholarships using the information provided in their admissions application. In addition, applicants have the chance to apply for donor and fellowship scholarships.

#5. Online MBA Scholarships at Open University

The Open University MBA is a unique distance learning program that aims to boost your business skills while you continue working. It offers you the opportunity to critically assess various perspectives and apply the knowledge you gain in real-world scenarios.

The program places a strong emphasis on practical learning that directly applies to management. The inclusion of ‘master’ in the degree title signifies the high level of expertise you’ll achieve in the field of management.

To attain this level of proficiency, the general MBA program highlights strategic analysis, interdisciplinary competencies, intellectual stimulation, and independent judgment. It covers essential topics such as critical engagement, theory integration into practical applications, and a global perspective on impact.

Requirements

Applicants will need to meet the following criteria to apply:

  • A bachelor’s degree awarded with honours from a recognised UK university or the overseas equivalent
  • A minimum of three years’ experience in a managerial, professional or technical role
  • Good knowledge of the English Language (to International English Language Test System (IELTS) score of 6.5)

#6. Online Baylor Online MBA Scholarships

For those with a business-oriented mindset, maximizing return on investment (ROI) is of utmost importance. Pursuing a Baylor MBA is a long-term investment that offers lifelong benefits, with results that manifest sooner than expected. Historically, over 90 percent of our recent MBA graduates found employment within 90 days of graduation, even in the early stages of their careers. Moreover, they secured an average starting salary exceeding $78,000. We understand that taking the first step to invest in your future can be daunting, which is why we provide financial assistance to the majority of our Full-Time MBA candidates.

Benefits of Baylor Online MBA Scholarships

  • Tuition Scholarships at Baylor

Baylor Scholarships encompass tuition costs and range from 25% to 100% tuition remission. These scholarships are granted based on criteria such as work experience, GMAT/GRE score, and academic record.

You do not need to submit a separate application for a scholarship. The Business School will notify you of any scholarship offer at the same time as your admission decision. Priority consideration is given to early applicants.

  • Graduate Assistantships at Baylor

The Business School provides several Graduate Assistant (GA) positions for MBA students. GAs usually work approximately 10 hours per week, supporting a faculty or staff member within the Business School. Upon acceptance of your application, you will have the chance to indicate your interest in working as a GA. The allocation of these positions is based on the order of received requests.
