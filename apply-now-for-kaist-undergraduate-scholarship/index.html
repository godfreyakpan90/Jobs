Apply Now for KAIST Undergraduate Scholarship 2024

If you’re interested in studying in South Korea, the KAIST University Undergraduate Scholarship presents an excellent opportunity. In this article, we will provide a comprehensive overview of the scholarship program, outlining its benefits and guiding you through the application process. This scholarship can pave the way for your undergraduate education at KAIST University.

The KAIST University Undergraduate Scholarship for 2024-2025 is a fully funded scholarship for international students, offering a fantastic opportunity for those pursuing their master’s studies. The scholarship includes various benefits, such as full tuition fee coverage, a monthly allowance of up to 800,000 KRW, one economy round trip, coverage for Korean language training fees, and medical insurance. It offers a comprehensive package to support your academic journey at KAIST University.

KAIST, the Korean Advanced Institute of Science and Technology, is renowned for its excellence in science and technology education. Often hailed as the “MIT of Asia,” the university has a strong reputation for research-focused programs and producing top-tier talent in these fields. The KAIST International Student Scholarship is a testament to the university’s commitment to attracting and supporting international students pursuing undergraduate and graduate studies. It provides a fantastic opportunity for those seeking a world-class education in South Korea.

Scholarship Coverage

The KAIST University Undergraduate Scholarship provides the recipient with the following benefits:

KAIST International Student Scholarship

  • Full tuition fee: tuition exemption for 8 semesters
  • Living expenses: 350,000 KRW per month
  • Medical Health insurance
Global Korea Scholarship
  • Full tuition fee: tuition exemption for 8 semesters
  • Living expenses: 800,000 KRW per month
  • Airfare: one economy round trip
  • Korean language training fee: one year
    • Korean language training is mandatory, and when the student does not achieve TOPIK grade 3 within a year, she or he is not eligible to enter the degree program.

Eligibility Criteria for KAIST University Undergraduate Scholarship

To qualify for the KAIST University Undergraduate Scholarship, candidates must fulfill all of the following requirements:

  • Required Language: English
  • Eligible Countries: All world countries. Applicants who hold Korean Citizenship (dual citizenship) are NOT eligible to apply as international students
  • For the spring semester of 2023 (EARLY only), candidates who have graduated or will graduate from their high school by February 28th, 2023
  • For the Fall Semester 2023, candidates who have graduated or will graduate from their high school by August 31st, 2023

How do I apply for a KAIST University undergraduate scholarship?

Please follow the following application instructions to apply for this scholarship.

(1) Go to the official site here and fill out your online application form.

(2) Pay the application fee (USD 80 or 80,000 KRW) by credit card or bank transfer.

(3) Upload scanned copies of the following additional required documents on the application page. The uploaded copies must be certified by school principal or the head or director of the organization and be clear and legible.

  • One recommendation letter
  • Secondary and tertiary school profiles
  • High school or university transcript
  • Standardized Official Test Report
  • English Proficiency Test Report
  • Passport or national ID
  • Statement of Financial Resources (KAIST Scholarship)
  • Statement of Financial Resources: Sponsorship
  • Honors and Awards (optional)

Note:

  • Please note that only ONE teacher recommendation letter will be considered for your application. The letter should be written by an academic advisor, homeroom teacher, or a math or science teacher. It is important that the letter is written in either Korean or English.
  • To ensure a smooth submission process, all recommendations must be submitted electronically via the online application platform. Once you have paid the application fee, we will send your teacher a link so they can submit your recommendation letter online before the deadline.
  • It is crucial to accurately enter your recommender’s email address on the online application form. This ensures they receive an automated email with instructions on how to proceed with their recommendation through the online process.
  • Your application will be considered complete once your recommender submits the recommendation letter. Please note that hard copies of recommendation letters will not be accepted.

If you encounter any issues while uploading your documents, you can mail them to the following address:

(4) Submit the application.
