Qatar Airways National Scholarship Program 2024

The Qatar Airways National Scholarship Program is currently accepting applications. In this article, we will provide a comprehensive overview of this scholarship program, including its advantages and a step-by-step guide to the application process.

The Qatar Airways National Scholarship Program for 2024 is a fully financed scholarship designed for Qatari nationals or individuals with a Qatari mother. This program encompasses support for undergraduate, master’s, and doctoral studies, with recipients receiving a monthly stipend and access to global opportunities, including internships within the Qatar Airways Group.

Welcome to a world where aspirations soar to new heights. Qatar Airways is in search of exceptional individuals to become part of its renowned team, comprising seasoned aviators and dynamic experts.

As part of its commitment to nationalization, Qatar Airways offers an inspiring National University Studies Scholarship Program that assists Qatari students in pursuing academic studies, both at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels, at educational institutions within the State of Qatar, or abroad. Qatar Airways provides opportunities for 23 different degree majors, encompassing a wide array of subjects ranging from aviation management to journalism to business.

Scholarship Coverage

Qatar Airways National Scholarship Program provides the recipient with the following benefits:

  • Monthly stipend.
  • Growth and learning possibilities with various training courses.
  • Global opportunities with internships at Qatar Airways Group
  • Annual academic reward for exceptional students.

Eligibility Criteria for Qatar Airways National Scholarship Program

To qualify for Qatar Airways National Scholarship Program, you must fulfill the following criteria:

  • Must be Qatari or have a Qatari mother.
  • Must be 18 years of age or older and must complete their degree by the age of 29.
  • Secondary School Education Certificate corresponding to the Ministry of Education and Higher Education grading scale with an average of 80% or higher
  • IELTS for Qatari: overall score of 5.5 or above, no less than 5 in each component.
  • ELTS for Qatari Mother: overall score of 6.0 or above, not less than 5.5 in each component.
  • Pass the interview set by Qatar Airways.
  • Current letter of unconditional offer from a university approved by the Ministry of Education and Higher Education in Qatar.
  • Graduated high school students must complete mandatory national service. However, the student who receives a confirmed offer letter from one of the universities listed in the Higher Education Emiri Scholarship programme will be exempt. Click here for a list.

How to Apply for Qatar Airways National Scholarship Program?

Please follow the following instructions to avail Qatar Airways National Scholarship Program:

  • Register here.
  • Upload the following documents:
    1. Resume / CV
    2. Copy of Highest Educational Certificate
    3. Copy of Passport
    4. Copy of English Proficiency Level
    5. Copy of National Service Clearance
    6. Copy of University Offer
