Best USA Scholarships

These scholarships are highly competitive and often require applicants to have outstanding academic achievements as well as other criteria such as leadership skills, community involvement, and extracurricular activities. Applying for scholarships can be a daunting task, but with the right approach and dedication, it is possible to secure funding for your education. Here are some of the best USA Scholarships.

Tide Scholarships

Tide Scholarships are designed to support undergraduate students from low-income backgrounds who are enrolled in a full-time course at an accredited institution. The scholarship covers tuition fees, living expenses, and books for the duration of the program. Tide Scholars also have access to mentorship programs and career development opportunities.

Scholarship Coverage

  • Tuition fees
  • Living expenses
  • Books and supplies

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Tide Scholarship, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Be enrolled in a full-time undergraduate program at an accredited institution.
  • Demonstrate financial need.
  • Have a minimum GPA of 3.0.
  • Be a citizen or permanent resident of the country where the scholarship is being offered.

How to Apply

Click here to begin your application for the Tide Scholarship.

Taco Bell Scholarship

The Taco Bell Foundation offers scholarships to students who demonstrate a passion for serving their community and are pursuing higher education. The scholarship covers tuition fees, books, and living expenses for up to four years.

Scholarship Coverage

  • Tuition fees
  • Living expenses
  • Books and supplies

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Taco Bell Scholarship, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Be a high school senior, graduate or current undergraduate student.
  • Have a minimum GPA of 3.0.
  • Demonstrate financial need.
  • Be actively involved in community service activities.

How to Apply

Interested students can apply for the Taco Bell Scholarship here. Applicants will be required.

Mike Rowe Scholarship

The Mike Rowe Scholarship, also known as the Work Ethic Scholarship Program, is offered to students who are interested in pursuing a career in the skilled trades. This scholarship covers full tuition and other related expenses for trade or technical programs.

Scholarship Coverage

  • Full tuition
  • Books and supplies
  • On-the-job training expenses

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Mike Rowe Scholarship, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Be a U.S. citizen or legal resident.
  • Be enrolled in or planning to enroll in an accredited trade or technical program.
  • Have a strong work ethic and passion for skilled trades.

How to Apply

Applications for the Mike Rowe Scholarship are available on their website. Applicants will be required to submit a short video explaining why they are passionate about pursuing a career in the skilled trades. Successful applicants will receive full tuition coverage and opportunities for on-the-job training with partnering companies. The Mike Rowe Scholarship aims to support students who are interested in careers that require technical skills and hands-on experience, but may not necessarily require a traditional four-year college degree.

Live Mas Scholarship

The Live Mas Scholarship, offered by Taco Bell, aims to empower the next generation of innovators and creators. This scholarship is open to students who demonstrate creativity and passion in pursuing their dreams.

Scholarship Coverage

  • Awards ranging from $5,000 to $25,000
  • Opportunities for mentorship and networking with Taco Bell executives

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Live Mas Scholarship, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Be a legal resident of the 50 United States and District of Columbia.
  • Be between the ages of 16-24 at time of application submission.
  • Submit a short video (2 minutes or less) showcasing creativity and passion in pursuing their dream.

How to Apply

Interested applicants can apply for the Live Mas Scholarship on their website. The application period typically opens in late January and closes in mid-February. Applicants will be required to submit a short video highlighting their passion and creativity, along with basic personal information and academic background.

FIU Scholarships for Skilled Trades

Florida International University (FIU) offers a range of scholarships specifically for students pursuing careers in the skilled trades. These scholarships aim to support and encourage students in their pursuit of technical skills and hands-on experience.

Scholarship Coverage

  • Awards ranging from $500 to $5,000
  • Opportunities for on-campus employment and internships with partnering companies

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for FIU’s skilled trades scholarships, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Be enrolled or accepted into a program related to the skilled trades at FIU.
  • Demonstrate financial need (as determined by FAFSA).
  • Maintain a minimum GPA of 2.5.

How to Apply

Interested applicants can apply for FIU’s skilled trades scholarships through the university’s scholarship portal. The application period typically opens in October and closes in January of each year.

Us Bank Scholarship

Us Bank is committed to supporting students in their pursuit of higher education through its scholarship program. The Us Bank Scholarship offers a range of opportunities for undergraduate and graduate students, including those studying in fields related to the skilled trades.

Scholarship Coverage

  • Awards ranging from $1,000 to $20,000
  • Renewable scholarships for up to four years

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Us Bank Scholarship, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Be enrolled or accepted into an accredited undergraduate or graduate program.
  • Demonstrate academic achievement and leadership potential.
  • Special consideration is given to underrepresented groups, including students studying in fields related to the skilled trades.

How to Apply

Interested applicants can apply for the Us Bank Scholarship through the Us Bank website. The application period typically opens in November and closes in February of each year. Be sure to check the official website for specific dates and guidelines.

College Board Opportunity Scholarships

The College Board Opportunity Scholarships program is a unique scholarship opportunity that rewards students for their efforts throughout high school, including completing college planning steps such as creating a college list, practicing for the SAT, and completing the FAFSA.

Scholarship Coverage

  • Awards ranging from $500 to $40,000
  • Over 25 scholarships awarded every week

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the College Board Opportunity Scholarships, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident.
  • Enroll in an accredited college or university in the fall following high school graduation.
  • Complete all six scholarship steps and earn at least 6 out of 12 possible entries.

How to Apply

Interested applicants can sign up for the College Board Opportunity Scholarships on the program’s official website. Eligible students will automatically be considered for scholarships based on their efforts throughout high school. The scholarship steps can be completed at any time during high school, with a deadline of February 28th of the student’s senior year. Be sure to check the official website for more information and guidelines on how to complete each step.

ELKS Most Valuable Student Scholarship

The ELKS Most Valuable Student Scholarship is a highly prestigious and competitive scholarship offered to high school seniors who display outstanding leadership, community service, and academic achievement.

Scholarship Coverage

  • Awards range from $1,000 to $12,500 per year for four years

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the ELKS Most Valuable Student Scholarship, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Be a current high school senior and U.S. citizen
  • Display strong leadership skills and community involvement
  • Have a minimum GPA of 3.0

How to Apply

Eligible students can apply for the ELKS Most Valuable Student Scholarship by completing an online application on the scholarship’s official website. The application includes submitting a transcript, SAT or ACT scores, and letters of recommendation. The deadline for submission is November 15th of the student’s senior year. Applicants are encouraged to start their application early to allow time for all required materials to be submitted by the deadline. Recipients will be notified in April of their senior year. For more information and guidelines, visit the scholarship’s official website. End of Document.

KU Scholarships

The University of Kansas offers a wide range of scholarships to undergraduate students, including those for international students. These scholarships are awarded based on academic merit, financial need, and other criteria.

Scholarship Coverage

  • Awards vary in amount and can cover tuition, fees, room and board, books, and other educational expenses

Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility requirements for University of Kansas scholarships vary depending on the specific scholarship. Some may require a certain GPA, while others may focus on financial need or particular areas of study. International students are also eligible for some scholarships.

How to Apply

To apply for University of Kansas scholarships, students must first complete the general admissions application and be accepted into the university. Some scholarships may require additional applications or materials, such as essays or letters of recommendation. Deadlines also vary, so it’s important to check each scholarship’s requirements and deadlines carefully.

Scholarship Opportunities

  • Incoming freshmen can apply for the Jayhawk Generations Scholarship, which is awarded based on academic achievement and financial need.
  • The Global Impact Scholarship is available to international students who have demonstrated a commitment to global issues and cross-cultural understanding.
  • The Smith Scholarship is specifically for students in the School of Business who have an entrepreneurial spirit and strong leadership skills.
  • Other scholarships are available for specific majors, such as engineering, journalism, or education.

For a comprehensive list of all scholarship opportunities at the University of Kansas, visit the financial aid website. End of Document.

Bryan Cameron Scholarship

  • This scholarship is for high school students who have a minimum GPA of 3.7 and are involved in extracurricular activities, community service, and leadership roles.
  • It covers full tuition for up to four years of undergraduate study at the University of Kansas.
  • Applicants must also demonstrate financial need and be a resident of one of the eligible states or Washington D.C.
  • The application opens in October and closes on December 1st.

For more information and to apply, visit the Bryan Cameron Foundation website. End of Document

The Chancellors Club Scholarship

  • This scholarship is awarded to outstanding incoming freshmen based on academic achievement, leadership experience, community service, and financial need.
  • Recipients receive $10,000 annually for up to four years of undergraduate study at the University of Kansas.
  • Applications are due in November and recipients are notified by March.

For more information and to apply, visit the Chancellors Club Scholarship website. End of Document

Honors Scholarships

  • Incoming freshmen who have been accepted into the University Honors Program may be eligible for additional scholarship opportunities through the program.
  • These scholarships range from $500 to full tuition and are based on academic achievement, leadership experience, and community service.
  • The application opens in October and closes on December 1st.

For more information and to apply, visit the University Honors Program website. End of Document

Global Scholars Program

  • This scholarship is for international students who have been admitted to the University of Kansas.
  • It covers full tuition, fees, and housing for up to four years of undergraduate study.
  • Applicants must have a minimum GPA of 3.75 and demonstrate outstanding academic achievement, leadership experience, and community involvement.
  • The application opens in September and closes on November 1st.

For more information and to apply, visit the Global Scholars Program website.
