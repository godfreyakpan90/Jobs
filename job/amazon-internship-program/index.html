The estimated total pay for a Amazon Internship is $35 per hour. The estimated base pay is $27 per hour.

The Amazon Internship Program is currently open for applications from Applicants with any level of education. The applicant can submit their resumes for consideration for this paid internship with Amazon.

How Does It Work?

Interns are given projects to own and finish with direction from managers, mentors, and teammates that have an influence on consumers right from the beginning of their time with the company. Interns complete their experience with Amazon by going through a full wrap-up process, which may involve presenting their project(s) to Amazon staff as part of their assessment.

What Does Amazon Look for An Intern?

Amazon hires students from all backgrounds.

Amazon internships typically occur between junior years for undergraduate students and between one year or 2 years for graduate students.

Educational background and work experience are assessed, but evaluations are also based on our Leadership Principles.

What Roles Does Amazon Recruit Students For?

We recruit and hire students into more than 100 roles across all business lines, including Amazon Studios and Prime Video; Amazon Web Services (AWS); Consumer, Devices, and Services; Human Resources; and Operations.

Some roles include software development engineers, support engineers, product managers, operations managers, applied scientists, vendor managers, and financial analysts.
